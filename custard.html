Custard

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups whole milk
  • 1/2 cup white sugar
  • 1/8 teaspoon salt
  • 3 eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Cinnamon sugar

Instructions:

  1.  Preheat oven to 325 degrees F.
  2.  Blend milk, sugar, and salt.
  3.  Beat in the eggs, then stir in the vanilla.
  4.  Pour into six custard cups.
  5.  Place cups in a pan of water.
  6.  Bake in oven for 50 minutes.
  7.  After 50 minutes, sprinkle with cinnamon sugar and return to oven for 10 more minutes.
  8.  Remove from water and let cool before refrigerating.
