Nancy Levin's Cheesecake

Ingredients:

  • 1 box graham cracker crumbs (use about 3/4 of the box
  • 1 1/2 sticks sweet butter
  • 2 1/2 cup sugar (1/2 cup for crust, 2 cups for cake, 4 tablespoons for topping)
  • 8 eggs at room temperature
  • 6 large packages Philadelphia cream cheese at room temperature
  • 1 pint sour cream
  • 4 1/2 tablespooons vanilla (2 1/2 tablespoons for cake and 2 tablespoons for topping)

Instructions:

  1.  Preheat oven to 375 degrees
  2.  Pour crumbs into a bowl and add butter and 1/2 cup of sugar
  3.  Mix with hands-stick onto bottom and sides of 10" springform pan
  4.  Separate white and yellows of eggs
  5.  Whip egg whites stiff
  6.  In separate bowl mix egg yolks and low speed
  7.  Add cream cheese little at a time
  8.  Add 2 cups sugar
  9.  After mixed well add 2 1/2 tablespoons vanilla
  10.  After blended fold in the whipped egg whites
  11.  Pour all over crumbs in form and bake 55 minutes
  12.  Preheat oven to 475
  13.  Mix 1 pint sour cream with 4 tablespoons sugar and 2 tablespoons vanilla
  14.  Sppon very carefully over cake and bake 10 minutes more at 475
  15.  After finished cool about 1 hour and out in refrigerator for at least 10 hours with foil around form and on top
  16.  Before serving decorate with strawberries
