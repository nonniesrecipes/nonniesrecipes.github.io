Rugelach

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup sweet butter
  • 1/2 lb. cream cheese (Philadelphia)
  • 2 cups flour
  • Cinnamon sugar
  • Ground walnuts
  • Golden Raisins
  • Apricot jam and raspberry jam

Instructions:

  1.  Blend butter, cream cheese, and flour together with your hands.
  2.  Mix it well until you form a ball of dough.
  3.  Divide dough into 4 pieces and form each into a small ball.
  4.  Wrap each piece in waxed paper and refrigerate overnight in a plastic bag.
  5.  Prepare shaker of cinnamon sugar.
  6.  Preheat oven to 375 F.
  7.  Flour counter and roll out each ball of dough to a round.
  8.  Sprinkle generously with cinnamon sugar and ground walnuts.
  9.  Cut into 12 pieces like a pie.
  10.  Place a 1/2 teaspoon of jam on each wedge near the edge, and then add 2-3 raisins on each.
  11.  Roll each wedge towards the pointed ends. Turn corners to make a crescent.
  12.  Place on a greased cookie sheet and sprinkle with cinnamon sugar.
  13.  Bake for 25 minutes.
  14.  Let cool on a plate...do not leave them to cool on the cookie sheet.
