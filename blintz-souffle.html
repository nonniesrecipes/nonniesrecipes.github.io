Blintz Souffle

Ingredients:

  • 8 frozen or fresh cheeese blitzes (thawed)
  • 1/4 cup (1/2 stick) butter, melted
  • 6 eggs
  • 2 cups sour cream
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla
  • 1/4 cup orange juice
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1 1/2 to 2 teaspoons salt

Instructions:

  1.  Preheat oven to 350 degrees
  2.  Arrange blintez on bottom of 2 quart casserole
  3.  Spood melted butter over them
  4.  Combine remaining ingredients in a large,deep bowl
  5.  Beat until light and smooth
  6.  Pour mixture over blintzes
  7.  Bake in oven for 1 hour
  8. Serve at once
  9. Serve with frozen strawberries in syrup (defrosted)
