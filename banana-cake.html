Banana Cake

Ingredients:

  • 1 stick butter
  • 1 1/4 cups sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 1/3 cups flour
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 cup ripe bananas
  • 4 tablespoons sour cream
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla

Instructions:

  1.  Preheat oven to 350 degrees
  2.  Cream butter and sugar
  3.  Beat in eggs
  4.  Mix in flour, baking soda, and salt
  5.  Mash up bananas and add to the mix
  6.  Mix in sour cream and vanilla
  7.  Add batter to greased rectangular pan
  8.  Bake for 30 minutes
