Coffee Cake

Ingredients:

  • 4 eggs separated
  • 1/2 pould sweet butter
  • 2 cups sugar
  • 3 cups flour
  • 4 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla

Instructions:

  1.  Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. Note this is a heavy batter and mixes best by hand...
  2.  Beat yolks, butter, and sugar well.
  3.  Add flour, baking powder, and vanilla. Dough will be sort of rubbery.
  4.  Beat egg whites stiff and fold into the above mixture.
  5.  Grease a rectangular pan and spread out dough.
  6.  Sprinkle will cinammon and sugar.
  7.  Drizzle little melted butter over top.
  8. %nbspBake at 350 for 1/2 hour.
