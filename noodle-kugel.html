Nonnie's Noodle Kugel

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 pound farmer cheese
  • 3 eggs
  • Small sour cream
  • 1/2 stick sweet butter
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla
  • 6 tablespoons sugar
  • 5 ounces Goodmans or Manischewitz broad egg noodles
  • Handful of golden raisins

Instructions:

  1.  Preheat oven to 350 degrees
  2.  Mix everything except the noodles and butter together
  3.  Boil and drain noodles
  4.  Melt butter and pour half on bottom of glass pyrex dish
  5.  Add rest of butter on top of mix
  6.  Bake at 350 for 1 hour and during last 10 minutes sprinkle cinnamon-sugar on top
    7. ***This is for a single recipe-use a square pyrex. I usually double the recipe and use a recgangular pyrex
